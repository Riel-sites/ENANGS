Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom design take to complete?

The timeline for a custom design varies depending on complexity and current workload. Typically, a complete outfit takes 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to final fitting. For wedding dresses, we recommend starting the process at least 6 months before your event.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is based on the complexity of the design, materials used, and time required. Simple designs start at $500, while more elaborate pieces like wedding gowns typically range from $2,000 to $10,000. We provide a detailed quote after our initial consultation.

Do you offer alterations for existing garments?

Yes, we offer alteration services for both our own creations and garments from other designers. Prices vary depending on the type of alteration needed. We recommend scheduling a consultation so we can assess the garment and provide an accurate estimate.

Can I provide my own fabric for a custom design?

Absolutely! We welcome clients who have special fabrics they'd like to incorporate into their designs. We'll assess the fabric during your consultation to ensure it's suitable for your desired garment and discuss any additional materials needed.

What is your sustainability policy?

We're committed to sustainable fashion practices. We source high-quality, eco-friendly materials whenever possible, minimize waste through careful pattern-making, and offer a garment care program to extend the life of your pieces. We also donate fabric scraps to local art programs.
